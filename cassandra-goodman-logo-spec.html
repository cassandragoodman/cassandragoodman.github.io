06 Clear space & minimum size
Cassandra Goodman
INDEPENDENT EDUCATIONAL CONSULTANT

Clear space. Keep a margin of at least x on all sides, where x = the cap-height of the wordmark. Nothing — text, edges, or other graphics — should enter this zone.

Minimum size. Print: 40 mm wide. Digital: 160 px wide. Below this the tagline becomes hard to read — drop the tagline or use initials instead.

07 Usage

Do

  • Scale the full lockup proportionally as one unit.
  • Use on white, off-white, or quiet neutral backgrounds.
  • Use the reversed version on dark or photographic backgrounds.
  • Give it generous clear space.

Don't

  • Recolor the wordmark or swap the gold for another hue.
  • Change the typefaces or add a third font.
  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or add shadows/outlines.
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
08 File deliverables checklist
